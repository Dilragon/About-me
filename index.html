Peter Thiel

Is a prominent entrepreneur, investor, and philanthropist best known as a co-founder of PayPal and Palantir Technologies. As the first outside investor in Facebook, Thiel has played a pivotal role in shaping the modern tech landscape. He is a founding partner of Founders Fund and has backed numerous groundbreaking startups through his venture capital efforts. Beyond business, Thiel is known for his thought leadership on innovation, technology, and politics, often challenging conventional views. He is also the author of the bestselling book Zero to One, which explores startup strategies and the future of progress.
